Our Story

Founded in 2020, Shamba Palace emerged from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by Kenyan farmers. Our founder, having grown up in a farming family in Chuka, witnessed firsthand how limited access to modern farming equipment held back agricultural progress in many communities. What started as a small equipment rental service with just two tractors has now grown into one of Kenya's leading agricultural machinery providers.

Through partnerships with local farming cooperatives and international equipment manufacturers, we've built a comprehensive fleet of modern machinery that serves hundreds of farmers across the country. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a steadfast commitment to our farming communities.

Our Mission

At Shamba Palace, our mission is to revolutionize agriculture in Kenya by providing affordable, efficient, and reliable machinery solutions to farmers. We aim to bridge the technology gap in farming, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity in rural communities while promoting environmentally conscious farming practices.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We build trust through transparent pricing, honest communication, and reliable service delivery.
  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in equipment quality and customer service, continuously training our team to exceed expectations.
  • Community: We actively participate in local agricultural initiatives, provide training workshops, and support farmer education programs.
  • Innovation: We continuously update our fleet with modern, fuel-efficient equipment and embrace digital solutions for easier booking and management.
  • Sustainability: We promote environmentally friendly farming practices and maintain our equipment to minimize environmental impact.
